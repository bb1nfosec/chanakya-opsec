# Security Policy

## Purpose

CHANAKYA is a **research and educational framework** for understanding OPSEC failures through signal correlation analysis.

## Intended Use

✅ **Permitted Uses:**
- Defensive security research and education
- OPSEC auditing of your own infrastructure
- Threat hunting and detection research
- Academic research and publication
- Lawful security testing with proper authorization

❌ **Prohibited Uses:**
- Unauthorized surveillance or monitoring
- Attacks against systems you don't own
- Violation of applicable laws or regulations
- Malicious use against individuals or organizations
- Commercial products without attribution

## Ethical Guidelines

1. **Obtain Authorization**: Only analyze systems you own or have explicit permission to audit
2. **Respect Privacy**: Don't use CHANAKYA to violate privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
3. **Responsible Disclosure**: If you discover vulnerabilities, follow responsible disclosure practices
4. **No Harm**: Don't use this framework to cause harm to individuals or organizations
5. **Legal Compliance**: Ensure your use complies with all applicable laws and regulations

## Reporting Security Issues

If you discover security or ethical issues with CHANAKYA itself:

1. **Do NOT** open a public GitHub issue
2. Email the maintainers privately (contact info in README.md)
3. Provide detailed information about the issue
4. Allow reasonable time for response (90 days)

We follow coordinated disclosure practices.
